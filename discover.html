Everything You Need

Located in the heart of Essex County wine country, with many wineries and breweries just minutes away. Take a 5 minute walk to Colchester Beach & Marina where there are many things to do like fish, play beach volleyball, or rent a paddle board. There is also a beautiful park to enjoy an ice cream while watching the boats coming and going from the marina!

On our property, there is a gazebo where you can enjoy a relaxing evening, or if you are lucky enough, experience the power of Lake Erie coming upon a storm.
