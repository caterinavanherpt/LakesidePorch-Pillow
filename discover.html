Everything You Need

Located in the heart of Essex County wine country, enjoy visiting wineries and a new brewery, just minutes away. Take a 5 minute walk to Colchester Beach & Marina where there are many things to do like fish, play beach volleyball, rent a paddle board or a paddle boat to cruise around on. For those who like to just sit and relax, it’s the perfect place. The gazebo is under renovation, but will be open mid summer for another area of relaxation at the water’s edge.
